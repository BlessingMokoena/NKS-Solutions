Our Background

NKS Solutions Institute is an engine of social change aimed at empowering people from different community backgrounds with digital literacy, Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR) skills development, academic research, social cohesion activities, and expertise in STEM education, coding, and robotics. We pride ourselves on producing excellent future Possibletarians who are constantly game changers in their respective communities.
